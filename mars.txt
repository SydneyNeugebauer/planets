One day many years ago, an eager student looked up at the sky and pondered which bright object 
might be Mars. Little did he know that one day he would be traveling to that mysterious red planet, 
among that vast field of stars. The seed planted at that one moment of pondering would bring about a 
change not only in his own life, but all of humanity, and shape the future as we live it today.
How did the dream of this one young man, shared by many throughout history, come to fruition? What 
series of events transpired to help him reach this great goal of humanity? We will explore this in 
the following chapters, and delve into the science and history that has propelled us to Mars and 
beyond.
